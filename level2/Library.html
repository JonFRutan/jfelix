Books

Crime and Punishment - Dostoevsky

This is a contender for the greatest book I've read. Dostoevsky delicately puts into words emotions we all feel but struggle to convey.

Crime and Punishment is the story of how misery warps the soul of a man into a monster. More importantly though, is Dostoevsky's message of hope, and that even at our worst there is redemption to be found.
